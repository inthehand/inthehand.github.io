Remarks
AcceptClient is a blocking method that returns a L2CapClient that you can use to send and receive data. Use the Pending method to determine if connection requests are available in the incoming connection queue if you want to avoid blocking.

Use the GetStream method to obtain the underlying Stream of the returned L2CapClient. The Stream will provide you with methods for sending and receiving with the remote host. When you are through with the L2CapClient, be sure to call its Close method.
